  • Evie Rogers

    French & Spanish

    I have recently completed my 1st year studying French and Spanish at the University of Bristol. I have always been a hardworking, committed and organised individual, achieving A*AA grades in my A-level exams. I have experience working with children; in 2014 I spent a month au-pairing for a French family in Lyon, France and last year I taught English to two teenage boys in Alicante, Spain.

    Biography

    I grew up in Hertfordshire, England. Annual holidays to France and Spain have fuelled my passion to learn their languages and understand more about their cultures and heritage.

    Personal Approach

    My love of knowledge is what makes me want to guide and shape the learning of other individuals. As an organised person, structure and discipline are important but I also want the tutee to feel comfortable to make suggestions at any time. I wish to ensure that classes are well-planned, engaging and enjoyable but also tailored to the needs and preferences of the student.

    When I’m not tutoring …

    I am an artistic and musical person. I play the piano and guitar, I write my own songs and I love going to concerts and festivals. Fitness and swimming is important to me, as well as spending time with family and friends.

  • Jack Ottoman

    French & Spanish

    I am currently in my second year, studying French and Spanish at the University of Bristol. Having studied them for many years, languages are something I feel very passionate about. I have previous experience tutoring younger students who struggled with their languages in my Secondary School.

    Biography

    Despite not being of any French or Spanish decent, I have been very lucky to have had several opportunities to visit both countries on several exchange trips and I spent a week in Spain working as a waiter. All of these opportunities really helped to enhance my linguistic abilities. I received an A* in both French and Spanish at GCSE Level as well as an A in A-Level French and an A* in Spanish.

    Personal Approach

    Studying both French and Spanish every day at university gives me a really good understanding of the languages which I feel would really help me when trying to address the problems which may come up with one of the students whilst tutoring. I can work individually with each student to try and get to the root of their potential struggles and try and overcome it in a way which suits them. I also believe having a passion for learning languages can only help in trying to get a message across to my students.

    When I’m not tutoring...

    I’m a huge football fan and thoroughly enjoy both watching from the stands and playing with my friends. I also watch a lot of films and TV programmes, both in English and French/Spanish.
