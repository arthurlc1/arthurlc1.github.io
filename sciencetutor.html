My studies have allowed me to deepen my understanding of both biology and chemistry and thus I am able to offer tutoring in these subjects at GCSE level.

    Biography

    I attended a state school and state sixth form and left with A*s in GCSE biology and chemistry as well as an A and A* at A Level respectively.     Personal approach

    Having captained my local football team for 4 years I have developed patience and an ability to motivate individuals towards a common goal. Having very recently left the secondary school education system I have up to date knowledge of the specifications and key tips and tricks required in order to achieve maximum marks. I also feel it is vital for the student to not only be able to answer questions, but to gain an understanding of the principles behind them.
