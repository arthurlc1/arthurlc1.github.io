About Us

By Students is a friendly tutoring and task providing service comprised of 100% University of Bristol Students, we offer tuition in all GCSE subjects, select A level subjects. We also offer tuition to first year students looking to get their foot in the door early. All of our tutors have gone through a rigorous application and interview process which have produced qualified, friendly, ambitious and organized tutors.

We believe students are a huge asset to any city. At Bristol University alone there 18,000 students, all with unique passions and talents. Within this huge pool of students any desired skill can be found. We aim to be the link that permits you to access the abundant supply of services our students can offer.
