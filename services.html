We believe students are a huge asset to any city. At Bristol University alone there 18,000 students, all with differnent passions and talents. Within this huge pool of students any desired skill can be found. We aim to be the link that permits you to access this great resource. Here we have a list of some of the services we can provide, however do not hesitate to contact us with any other jobs that may interest you.

  • Babysitting

    We offer parents the knowledge that their children are left in safe and trusted hands, which is achieved by the vetting process our babysitters must go through before they can register with us. Our babysitting can take place at any time at the parent’s home. We can also provide sitters for parents staying in local Bristol hotels.

  • Dog Walking

    All of our dog walkers are dog owners, they all know how important the little things are to your dog, whether it’s scratching their tickle spot or turning on the TV for them. We are privileged to look after your dog, and to ensure safety and that each dog gets the attention it deserves, we limit our walks to four dogs per walk.

  • Sports coaches

    The University of Bristol is a sports hub, with some of its alumni appearing in the recent 2016 Rio Olympics. UOB Students pride themselves on being able to balance hours of studying with hours of excercise each week. Our pool of physically gifted Students, can provide services varying anywhere from rigorous personal training sessions to casual tennis or swimming lessons.

  • Tech Support

    With one of our co founders being a computer scientist, we understand the importance of having someone tech savvy around. Whether you need help building a website, or just some technical support around the house our students can help you.
